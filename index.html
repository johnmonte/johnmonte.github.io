John Monte runs an independent design studio in the Philippines, collaborating with clients worldwide to grow their businesses through technology and good design.

Purveyor of fine handcrafted digital goods since 2004

I’ve been in the web industry for quite some time and I've had the privilege to work with startups, businesses, and agencies trusted by a few big names. I’ve collaborated mostly on visual design and interactions, but my experience has taught me how all the digital pieces come together to form a whole.

The know-how I learned in diverse fields can be employed interchangeably. What I do is so much more than crafting code or pushing pixels around until they look pretty. I think about the big picture, the small details, the people using the product, the technology, and the business.

Work file 01 / 03Monte TV — inspiring design fellows through knowledge sharing and curated videos

  • Filed under: Side project
  • Status: Work in progress
  • Role: Designer + Developer
  • Launch project ⟶

Monte TV is an online television for designers and non-designers who work with designers—showing videos about UX, UI, interaction, branding and product design. The idea started when I was creating a simple video playlist to share and I ended up turning it into a constant stream of design videos randomized with YouTube API and laid out in a TV format.
